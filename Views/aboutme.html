About Me

Hi there, I'm Buyan, born in China, just finished my master study at the University of Melbourne.

I studied in Master of IT(HCI), which is the Master of Information Technology(Human-Computer Interaction), I love it so much and I'm trying my best to make it a life-long passion.

My bachelor degree is in Computer Science and Technology, a very challenging major but with lots of fun. However, I found myself more interested in the user-side of things so I made a decision to study on human-computer interaction, from which I found the UX design very intriguing and I want to make it my next try, as a job.

Apart from that, I can do web development, basically on Nodejs and HTML/CSS, and thanks to HCI, I can also do design and prototyping, which is somehow related to user experience(UX) as well.

What I've done recently is a VR reading project, which involves a number of interaction between user and virtual space and objects. Seeing how people react to those novel applications and helping them gradually understand virtual reality is really a motivation for product design and development.

About my career arrangement, I've graduated now so I'm looking for UX designer or Interaction designer jobs.
